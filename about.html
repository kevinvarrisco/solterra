About Us

Founded in Bali more than 12 years ago, Solterra was established in response to the growing demand for reliable and professional villa management services in Bali's rapidly evolving hospitality industry. Recognizing the challenges faced by villa owners in maintaining operational quality, maximizing property potential, and delivering consistent guest experiences, Solterra was built to provide dependable management solutions grounded in professionalism, trust, and long-term partnership.
